About me

Hi there! I’m Ziyuan Huang (黄子渊), currently a Ph.D. candidate in Advanced Robotics Centre at National University of Singapore, supervised by Professor Marcelo Ang. My main research interests are on video understanding, including action recognition and localization, video representation learning, multi-modal learning, and video-based scene understanding.

Currently, I am a visiting student in the MARS Lab at Tsinghua University, supervised by Professor Zhao Hang, working at Qi Zhi Institute. The main research topic is video-based environment perception. I am fortunate to have been working closely with Dr. Liang Pan and Professor Ziwei Liu in S-Lab@NTU. Before my visit to MARS Lab, I have spent wonderful times conducting research in the Vision Lab@DAMO Academy, Alibaba Group, under Professor Rong Jin, and Vision4Robotics Group at Tongji University under Professor Changhong Fu.
