About Me

I am a PhD student from the School of Informatics in the University of Edinburgh, supervised by Dr. Chris Xiaoxuan Lu. Recently, I am taking my first-year study of CDT-RAS, hosted at the Edinburgh Centre for Robotics. My sponsor source is the School of Informatics funded CDT-RAS scholarship. Prior to this, I received my B.Eng degree with the highest honor Academic Star from Tongji Univeristy in Shanghai (2017-2021). During my bachelor study, I worked as a Research Assistant at the Vision4Robotics Lab mentored by Dr. Changhong Fu and did research at the Tsinghua University as a visiting student.

My research interests lie in i) robust perception for mobile autonomy under adverse conditions, which focuses on developing scene understanding algorithms based on emerging 4D automotive radar; ii) weakly (self-) supervised learning of deep neural network for different tasks, such as scene flow estimation, 3D multi-object detection and tracking. My previous research includes visual tracking, UAV localization and adversarial attack.
